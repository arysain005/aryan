I'm a mechanical engineer with a focus on thermal-fluid systems and lightweight structural design. I enjoy working at the intersection of simulation and physical prototyping — I'm not satisfied until the model and the hardware agree.

My background spans aerospace components, motorsport, and consumer robotics. I thrive in fast-moving teams where design decisions are driven by data, and I'm comfortable owning a project from initial concept through to manufacture and test.

When I'm not at a workbench or running an FEA mesh, I'm probably building something in the workshop or reading about additive manufacturing processes.
